All about SNPEuro

SNPEuro (Single Nucleotide Polymorphism Euro)is a web application that aims at retrieving SNP information for a genomic region of interest.

The database consists of SNPs from five European populations: British, Finnish, Iberian, Toscani and CEPH.

Users are able to select either a genomic coordinate, SNP name or a gene of interest.

The name, genomic position, genotype frequencies and allele frequencies for each SNP will be retrieved.
