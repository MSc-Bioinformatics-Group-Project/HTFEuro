Instructions on how to use SNPEuro

  • Choose the type of search you require.
  • Inputs available are SNP, Gene Name and Genomic Coordinates.
  • View the genetic annotations and download as a csv file.
  • For searches that result in more than one SNP being selected, you can select statistical tests of choice, populations and sliding window parameters.
  • You can view the statistical results from your selection and download them.
  • For SNP and gene searches, you can download genotype and allele frequencies. The output is shown below.
